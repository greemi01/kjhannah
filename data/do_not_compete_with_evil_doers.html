Do Not Compete with Evil Doers.

Retirement age is often about empty nesting and grandchildren. In some cases, it is equally about war, facing down international prejudices, and coping with increased disability. Likewise, getting older might bring along late applications of professional skills, newfound gratitude for being alive and for the small and large miracles that constitute daily existence, and a measure of nostalgia for personal and social history. Do Not Compete with Evil Doers explores all of these topics.
