Writing books is like following the course of a river toward a waterfall; words necessarily cascade. Sometimes, they fall over a great precipice. Always, they create a plungepool. No matter the rate at which they are produced or the manner in which they drop over the edge, fashioning their patterns is a pleasant pursuit.
