Sometimes, a single image can better convey meaning than an assemblage of words. While descriptions are invaluable, on occasion, complex notions can be most successfully communicated through pictures.

Consider the seven spectral colors as well as their amalgamation into rainbows. Each of these rubrics for visual representation can help us grasp our amazing world. If we appreciate Creation’s beauty, we can further understand our place within it. If we broaden our awareness of our station, we can live enhanced lives.
